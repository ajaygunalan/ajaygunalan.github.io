I completed my Bachelors's degree in Mechanical engineering(2013-2017). Right after my Bachelors, I joined [ASIMOV Robotics Pvt. Ltd, Kochi](https://www.asimovrobotics.com/) and was a part of the Software team, developing a [service robot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vjY1c8N1rk/) for HDFC bank and then I joined [RBCCPS](http://www.rbccps.org/) in February 2018 to work on legged robots and manipulators.

<br>

My interest is real-time software development for planning, and control, of robotics. More specifically on under-actuated system. I believe that to have an agile and dextrous robot, We must have an inter-disciplinary, broad-view, approach. Covering all the vital areas such as control, computation, learning, and hardware. Otherwise, we won't even know(guess) the reason of failure.
