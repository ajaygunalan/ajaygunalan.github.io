
I’m Ajay G from [Robert Bosch Center for Cyber-Physical System](http://www.rbccps.org/) at [Indian Institute of Science, Banglore](https://www.iisc.ac.in/). I took Mechanical Engineering because of the curiosity in how things move and work, from high school. During my engineering, I explored a lot
of fields and discovered that the real beauty of dynamics lies in the day-to-day activities we do, which can be realised only when
we try to make a robot to perform the same task. Suddenly what was so simple and trivial to us became hard. Though being
hard, it is an interesting challenge. To solve the problem, one will require various skills in Math, Computer, Electrical,
Mechanical, Controls, etc. Almost all the fields which are based upon on classical mechanics would be involved. In fact, we can
say that Robotics is the pinnacle of classical mechanics. Such a diverse field with a cool hardware aroused excitement and
passion for me to pursue research in robotics.

<br>


Thus, to quench my thirst for research, immediately after graduating in 2017, I joined [ASIMOV Robotics Pvt. Ltd, Kochi](https://www.asimovrobotics.com/) and
was a part of the software team(R&D), developing a [service robot](https://ajaygunalan.github.io/projects/past/asimov.html) for HDFC bank. Then I joined Robert Bosch Center for
Cyber-Physical System by February 2018 to work on [legged robots](https://ajaygunalan.github.io/projects/past/stoch.html) and manipulators under the guidance of [Dr. Shishir N.Y. Kolathya](https://shishirny.github.io/) and  [Prof. Bhardwaj Amrutur](http://chips.ece.iisc.ernet.in/index.php/Bharadwaj_Amrutur). I would call myself a **Full-Stack Roboticist** because I like to build software for
robots from scratch involving embedded systems, controls, planning, and machine learning. My research interest is to build
dexterous and agile under-actuated robots. To achieve it, I think we should have a broad inter-disciplinary approach in order
to accurately debug the failures. After all, I feel that, “Robotics is the research of engineering”.
